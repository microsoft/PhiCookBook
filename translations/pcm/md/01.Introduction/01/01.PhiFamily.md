# Phi family wey Microsoft get

Di Phi models na di most capable and cost-effective Small Language Models(SLMs) wey dey available, dem dey outperform models wey get di same size and even di next size up for plenty language, reasoning, coding, audio, vision, and math benchmarks. Dis release dey expand di selection of high-quality models for customers, dey offer more practical choices for composing and building generative AI applications.

Di Phi Family start with Phi-1 for Python Code generation, e continue to Phi-1.5 /2 wey base on text and chat completion, Phi-3-mini/small/medium-instruct and Phi-3.5/4-mini-instruct, and e develop reach Phi-3/3.5-vision for vision, Phi-4 wey base on strong reasoning, and Phi-3.5-MoE for MoE, and now di full-modal model Phi-4-multimodal. Through high-quality data sets, benchumark fit train to be comparable to models wey get larger training parameters.

## Example of How to Choose Model

| | | | |
|-|-|-|-|
|Wetin customer need|Task|Start wit|More Details|
|Need model wey go just summarize a thread of messages|Conversation Summarization|Phi-3 / 3.5 text model|Di deciding factor na say di customer get well-defined and straightforward language task|
|Free math tutor app for pikin dem|Math and Reasoning|Phi-3 / 3.5 / 4 text models|Because di app na free, customers wan solution wey no go dey cost dem on a recurring basis |
|Self Patrol Car Camera|Vision analysis|Phi-3 /3.5 -Vision or Phi-4-multimodal|Need solution wey fit work on edge without internet|
|Dem wan build AI based travel booking agent|Needs complex planning, function calling and orchestration|GPT models|Need ability to plan, call APIs to gather information and execute |
|Dem wan build copilot for their employees|RAG, multiple domain, complex and open ended|GPT models + Phi Family |Na open-ended scenario, e need broader world knowledge, so bigger model dey more suited. You go need to chunk the knowledge content, maybe SLM go dey good for you |
